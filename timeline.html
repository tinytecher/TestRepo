Get to know me.


As a UX/UI designer, researcher, and software developer, with a background in business psychology I enjoy delivering applications that exceed client expectations and delight the user.

UX/UI Design & Research

Understanding and empathising with the user through researching their needs and goals is pivotal in my work. Seeing the user as the heartbeat of the project, I ensure they are front and centre at every stage from ideation, through design to final product delivery.

With an in-depth understanding of the user problem and evaluating the best route to solving it, I find it really rewarding developing user personas, stories and journeys, and creating wireframe designs to develop products that are enjoyable, easy to use, accessibile and inclusive for all users. By delivering ongoing user-testing of prototypes and products the user experience is optimised.

Agile Team Working

Enjoy working in agile teams, communicating and managing workflows in real-time using tools such as Trello, Kanban and Miro.

Participating in and facilitating agile sprints, stand-ups and retrospectives, enables me to keep the team and project on track, troubleshoot issues and continuously reflect to maximise the best possible outcomes and enhance productivity.

Software Development

Experience of software development and pair programming gained during an intensive four month full stack web development bootcamp. Designed, developed and deployed entire user-driven web applications.

Worked in agile teams on projects, including a 1 month full-time assignment designing and building an event planning web application. Tech stack includes JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3, ReactJS, NextJS, Postman, SQL, PostgresSQL, Heroku, Netlify, NodeJS, ExpressJS, Testing using Cypress & Jest.

Business Psychology & Research

As free-lance consultant I have enjoyed conducting a wide range of research, product development and assessment assignments for public and private sector clients. My mission is to help organisations and people get the best from each other.

Working as an associate with partner consultancies, I enjoy making a difference to businesses, teams and the individuals within them. Some of my areas of expertise include designing and running focus groups, surveys, 360 degree feedback and presenting research results.

Training

As a Tech Product Training Specialist, I get pleasure enabling and supporting clients learning to use web platforms and mobile apps.

With experience in various training roles within businesses, public services and educational settings, I enjoy collaborating in teams, supporting both colleagues and trainees.

Business Management

Managing all business areas of a high-end domestic housekeeping franchise, I delivered a top quality, highly trusted service to clients in their own homes.

Single-handedly led sales and marketing, finance, operations, HR, recruitment, team management and training, admin and customer relationships.
