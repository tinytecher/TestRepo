UX/UI Design & Research

Establishing empathy for the user through researching their needs and goals is pivotal in my work. Seeing the user as the heartbeat of the project, I ensure they are front and centre at every stage from ideation, through design to final product delivery.

With an in-depth understanding of the user problem and evaluating the best route to solving it, I find it really rewarding developing user personas, stories and journeys, and creating wireframe designs to develop products that are enjoyable, easy to use, accessibile and inclusive for all users. By delivering ongoing user-testing of prototypes and products the user experience is optimised.
